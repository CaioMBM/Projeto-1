Contact Support

Support hours are Monday-Friday 05:00-18:00 Pacific Time. We observe all U.S. holidays. Unable to sign in? Reach us here.

Contact support

  1. While signed in to Abstract, select the icon.
    • If you're in the Abstract web app, the icon is in the lower left corner.
    • If you're in the Abstract Desktop app, the icon is in the upper right corner.
  2. Select Contact Support.
  3. On the Contact Support page, choose the Topic you want to contact Support about, write a Subject for your message, and enter a Description of your issue.
  4. If you have files you want to give Support, such as bug reports or screenshots, click Browse files... and choose them from your computer.
  5. When you're done, click Send to Support.
